Diabetes is the leading cause of death in the world, and it also affects kidney disease,
loss of vision, and heart disease. Data mining techniques contribute to health care decisions
for accurate disease diagnosis and treatment, reducing the workload of experts. Diabetes
prediction is a rapidly expanding field of research. Early diabetes prediction will result in
improved treatment. Diabetes causes a variety of health issues. Therefore, it is critical to
prevent, monitor, and raise awareness about it.
